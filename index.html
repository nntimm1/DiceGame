How to Play

Gameplay

In the first three rounds, the lowest two players each round are removed
from the game leaving 4 players in the game.
In rounds 4 and 5, the lowest player is removed from the
game leaving 2 players in the game.
In the 6th and final round, a shootout occurs between the final two players.

Scoring

The objective of the game is to be the last player standing!
